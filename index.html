        Cloud Sync (Google Drive)

        Back up automatically to your own Google Drive and sync between your iPhone and iPad. Your data is saved to a LostWeightNow folder in your Drive. Nothing is stored anywhere else.

        One-time setup — see the steps in the app's README to create this in Google Cloud (free).

        AI Coach (Google Gemini)

        Optional. When on, each meal is sent to Google Gemini for richer, personalised coaching; the built-in coach is used offline or if this fails. Get a free API key at aistudio.google.com.

        Your Data

        Your data is stored on this device and (if connected) synced to your Google Drive. Export backup saves a JSON file — including your Drive Client ID, Gemini API key and coach persona — which you can keep in Files / iCloud and re-import on another device. Keep that file private, as it contains your keys.
